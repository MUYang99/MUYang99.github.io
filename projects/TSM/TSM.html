The Tree Species Distribution dataset of Germany

Yang Mu, Jianhua Guo, Muhammad Shahzad, Xiao Xiang Zhu

Study area

The Federal Republic of Germany has a total of 11.4 million hectares of forest and a substantial forest stock volume of 3.7 × 109 cubic meters. Fig.1 shows the specific location.

Workflow of this study

There are five main stages: satellite data acquisition, label generation, training data preparation, model training and mapping.

Training Networks and Pytorch Codes

The proposed ForestFormer integrates a dual-branch network architecture, complemented by attention mechanisms from the Transformer model in both spectral and spatial domains.

The pytorch code of this work is available from TSM_master.

Tree Species Distribution dataset of Germany dataset download
