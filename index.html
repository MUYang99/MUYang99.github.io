KTH-GEO Wildfire Monitor
link / code

Advisor: Prof. Dr. Yifang Ban, KTH Royal Institute of Technology
Developed python programs to automatically download Sentinel 1/2 data, process Sentinel-2 Optical and Sentinel-1 GRD/SLC SAR images based on Snappy library and upload processed data into Google Earth Engine. The final results can be used to monitor wildfire progressions in the western regions of the U.S. and Canada.

Research on the land use of Wuhan city based on Deep Learning (Bachelor thesis)
slides / code

Advisor: Prof. Dr. Shugen Wang, Wuhan University
This research is to use different deep learning models (AlexNet, VGG11, ResNet-50 and ResNet-101) to classify remote sensing images of Wuhan at the pixel level into 11 classes, and to analyze the landscape pattern of the city with Fragstats based on the results.
